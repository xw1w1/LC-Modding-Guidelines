## How to enable snow footprints on your moon

### 1. In your `SelectableLevel` script, tick "`Level Includes Snow Footprints`".
<img width="511" height="82" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ff6127f2-9622-4eb8-b8a1-241f02ec58f4" />

### 2. From any snowy level's Scene, copy `Systems/SnowFootprints` and paste it into your Scene's `Systems`.
<img width="463" height="277" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/07cf82e7-6e9c-40c8-9073-6e15d61c7c94" />

### 3. Go to your `Terrain` and set it's Tag to "`Snow`".
<img width="534" height="144" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b461e4cb-5fcf-496b-8b60-a6bbce88f84c" />


well that's all !
